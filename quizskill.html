Quiz - Developing New Skills

LET'S PRACTICE WHAT YOU LEARNED!

Question 1: What’s the first step in developing a skill you’re passionate about?
A) Ignore what you enjoy
B) Try different activities to see what you like
C) Wait for someone to tell you what to do
D) Only do things you’re already good at

Question 2: How can exploring your interests and learning new skills impact your financial success later?
A) It gives you more opportunities in life
B) You don’t need to learn new things
C) It doesn't affect your future income
D) It won’t make any difference

Question 3: How do you get better at a new skill?
A) Work hard and practice regularly
B) Just do it once
C) Stop if it gets difficult
D) Only work when it’s fun

Question 4: Which of these is an example of a skill you can develop?
A) Playing video games all day
B) Sitting around doing nothing
C) Watching TV
D) Learn coding

Question 5: How can turning your passion into a side hustle benefit your finances?
A) It is a way to avoid working hard
B) It distracts you from your main career goal
C) It provides an additional source of income that you can use for saving or investing
D) It makes budgeting unnecessary
