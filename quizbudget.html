A) Spend all your money first
B) List all your income and expenses
C) Start saving without a plan
D) Buy whatever you need, then figure it out

Question 2: What is the 50/30/20 rule in budgeting?
A) Spend 50% on needs, 30% on savings/investments, 20% on current/future wants
B) Spend 50% on current/future wants, 30% on needs, 20% on savings/investments
C) Spend 50% on needs, 30% on current/future wants, 20% on savings/investments
D) Spend 50% on savings/investments, 30% on current/future wants, 20% on needs

A) So you can make sure you spend all your money
B) To see how much debt you can accumulate
C) To ensure you are staying within your budget
D) To know how much fun you’ve had

A) Ignore it and keep spending
B) Use your credit card for everything
C) Cut back on non-essential expenses
D) Ask for more allowance

A) Spend all your money as soon as you get it
B) Save whatever is left at the end of the month
C) Set aside savings at the start of the month
