Quiz - Passive Income

LET'S PRACTICE WHAT YOU LEARNED!

Question 1: What is passive income?
A) Money earned without any work at all
B) Money earned from a regular job
C) Money earned with minimal ongoing effort after an initial setup
D) Income earned from doing chores

Question 2: Which of the following is an example of passive income?
A) Working a part-time job at a restaurant
B) Receiving interest from a savings account
C) Babysitting for neighbors
D) Doing freelance work

Question 3: How can you start earning passive income?
A) By creating engaging content and posting at Youtube
B) By starting a business that requires daily involvement
C) By playing video games all day
D) By spending all your money on fun things

Question 4: What is one key benefit of passive income?
A) You can stop working entirely once you start earning it
B) It provides financial security and extra income while you're still in school or working
C) It allows you to buy everything you want without a budget
D) It requires no planning or strategy

Question 5: Which of these activities could potentially become a passive income stream?
A) Walking dogs for your neighbors
B) Mowing lawns every week
C) Taking a vacation
D) Writing an eBook and selling it online
