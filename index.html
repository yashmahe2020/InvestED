Welcome to InvestED

Your ultimate guide to mastering money

Our school education system often overlook essential life skills, especially financial literacy. InvestED fills that gap, empowering teens and young adults to take control of their financial future. InvestED is a free interactive financial literacy app developed by Yash Maheshwari, a high school student, to provide financial education in a fun and practical way.

Learn essential skills through interactive lessons, case studies, quizzes, and mock investment tools, designed for everyone.

Financial literacy is the key to unlocking a secure financial future.

Why InvestED Works

Goal-Oriented Learning

InvestED encourages users to set and track financial goals—whether it’s saving for a big purchase, learning about investments, or preparing for future. This helps users see the direct impact of financial literacy on achieving personal milestones.

Builds Smart Money Habits Early

InvestED app teaches teens how to budget, save, invest and manage money from a young age. These habits, formed early, can set them up for long-term financial success, helping them avoid common mistakes like overspending or falling into debt.

Boosts Confidence and Independence

Gaining financial literacy boosts a teen's confidence in handling money, giving them a sense of independence and control over their finances. It empowers them to make smarter financial choices and set goals for their future.

Prepares for Real-World Challenges

With interactive case studies and simulations, youth can practice real-world financial scenarios, practice investing and financial decision making without risking real money—so they’re ready to handle money independently.
